üîç 5 Key most importatnd  Steps to Verify a Payment


        


            

                
1. Check Bank SMS Alert

                
The bank‚Äôs SMS is the ONLY confirmation of payment. No SMS = No payment.

            


            

                
2. Check Balance in Official Bank App

                
Open your bank app and refresh the balance. If the amount is not credited, the payment is fake.

            


            

                
3. Verify UPI Transaction ID (UTR)

                
Real UPI payments have a unique 12-digit UTR. Check if the UTR appears in your own UPI app or bank statement.

            


            

                
4. Look for UI Errors in Fake Apps

                
Fake apps usually have spelling mistakes, poor layout, or missing animations.

            


            

                
5. Ask for Live Scroll

                
Ask the sender to scroll through transaction history and open details.
